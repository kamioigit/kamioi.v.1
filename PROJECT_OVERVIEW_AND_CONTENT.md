# Kamioi - Project Overview & Content Summary

## üéØ What is Kamioi?

**Kamioi** is an AI-powered automatic investing platform designed for **Gen Z and college students**. The core concept is simple: **"Own What You Buy"** - every purchase you make automatically becomes stock ownership in that company.

### Core Value Proposition
- **Link your bank account once** ‚Üí Upload your bank statement, no apps to download, no cards to link
- **Live your life normally** ‚Üí Buy coffee, Netflix, shoes, tech, entertainment - do whatever you normally do
- **Automatic stock ownership** ‚Üí AI analyzes every transaction and turns your spending into stock ownership
- **Set it and forget it** ‚Üí No daily check-ins, no manual decisions, just automatic wealth building

### Target Audience
- **Primary**: Gen Z (ages 18-27)
- **Secondary**: College students and young professionals
- **Pain Point**: Want to invest but don't want the complexity, time, or knowledge required

### How It Works
1. **Link Once**: Upload bank statement (one-time setup, 2 minutes)
2. **Live Your Life**: Continue normal spending (coffee, streaming, shopping, etc.)
3. **Own Automatically**: AI maps purchases to stocks and invests automatically
